# Steering Knuckle & Hub Design (Autodesk Fusion 360)

## Description
Designed and modeled a steering knuckle and hub assembly using Autodesk Fusion 360 as part of a mechanical design project.

## Tools Used
- Autodesk Fusion 360

## Objective
To understand the design and working of steering system components used in automobiles.

## Key Features
- 3D modeling of steering knuckle
- Hub assembly design
- Accurate dimensioning
- Proper component alignment

## My Role
Independently designed the complete 3D model of the steering knuckle and hub assembly focusing on structural accuracy and functional design.

## Outcome
Successfully developed a CAD model demonstrating knowledge of automotive components and mechanical design principles.
